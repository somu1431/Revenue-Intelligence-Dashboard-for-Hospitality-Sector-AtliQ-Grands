**Project Objective:**

Develop an interactive **Power BI dashboard** for AtliQ Grands, a premium hotel chain, to analyze key hotel performance metrics such as **Revenue, Occupancy, ADR,** and **Booking Trends.**

Help the Revenue Management Team identify the reasons behind declining market share and take **data-driven actions** to improve performance.

**Purpose of the Dashboard:**

Provide a **unified view of hotel performance**that enables management to monitor business KPIs, detect patterns, and optimize revenue strategies across booking platforms, cities, and room types.

**Importance of Revenue Analytics:**

Identify **low-performing markets** and take corrective actions.

Monitor **booking patterns** (weekday vs weekend, platform-based).

Improve pricing, **occupancy,** and **revenue** strategies through data insights.

**Role of Reports:**

Help management track **key KPIs** like RevPAR, ADR, and Occupancy %.

Provide **city-wise and platform-wise performance tracking.**

Deliver **actionable insights** to guide marketing and pricing decisions.

Enable the team to achieve a **20%** recovery in revenue and market share within a month.

**Technical & Soft Skills:**

**Technical Skills:**

Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.

Created Fact and Dimension tables for efficient data modeling.

Designed KPIs using DAX measures (Revenue, ADR, RevPAR, Occupancy %).

Used slicers, bookmarks, and dynamic visuals for interactivity.

Applied dashboard storytelling principles for clear communication.

Published and shared live reports through Power BI Service.

**Soft Skills:**

Translating business requirements into analytical reports.

Collaborating with stakeholders to refine report insights.

Applying critical thinking to detect performance gaps.

Presenting data visually for better decision-making.

**Key Insights & Outcomes:**

Built a **live interactive dashboard** to analyze bookings, day type, and platform performance.

Pinpointed the reasons behind revenue decline and poor occupancy.

Enabled the Revenue Team to take corrective action, leading to a **predicted 20% increase in revenue and market share.**

Delivered a scalable BI solution for ongoing performance tracking.
